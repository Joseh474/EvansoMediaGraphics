About Evanoh Media & Graphics

Professional Design Solutions for the Modern Business

Who We Are

Evanoh Media and Graphics is a professional design agency specializing in comprehensive visual communication solutions. Established in 2020, we have built a reputation for delivering high-quality design services that meet the evolving needs of businesses, organizations, and individuals across Kenya and beyond.

We provide end-to-end design solutions encompassing digital graphics, print media, brand identity development, and video content creation. Our approach combines strategic thinking with creative execution to ensure that every project not only meets aesthetic standards but also achieves measurable business objectives.

Operating at the intersection of creativity and commerce, we understand that effective design is a critical business asset. Our commitment to excellence, attention to detail, and client-focused methodology has established us as a trusted partner for organizations seeking professional visual communication solutions.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional design solutions that empower businesses and individuals to communicate effectively, build strong brand identities, and achieve their strategic objectives through innovative visual media.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a leading creative design agency in Africa, distinguished by our commitment to quality, innovation, and client success. We aspire to set industry standards for professional design services.

Our Commitment

We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of professionalism, delivering projects on time and within budget, and building long-term partnerships based on trust, quality, and consistent results.

Our Process

A structured approach to delivering exceptional results

1. Consultation & Discovery

We begin with an in-depth consultation to understand your business objectives, target audience, brand guidelines, and project requirements. This ensures alignment from the outset.

2. Strategic Planning

Based on our consultation, we develop a comprehensive project plan outlining timelines, deliverables, and key milestones to ensure smooth execution.

3. Design & Development

Our design process combines creativity with technical precision. We develop initial concepts and refine them based on your feedback to achieve the perfect solution.

4. Review & Refinement

We present our work for your review and make necessary refinements to ensure the final product meets your expectations and project requirements.

5. Delivery & Support

Final files are delivered in all required formats with comprehensive documentation. We provide ongoing support to ensure successful implementation.
